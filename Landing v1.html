The maker · 008

Hi, I'm Jeff.

I've been drawing portraits of strangers at Singapore markets for eight years under the name Playing With Pencil. This is what happened when I taught my pencil to use a camera.

Every sketch I do is two things at once: a likeness, and a memory. I wanted a photobooth that did both — something that catches you faithfully and wraps you in a little bit of nostalgia. So I built one. Hardwood tripod, brass fittings, and an AI model I fine-tuned on forty years of film photographs. Six months of tinkering later, Playing With Photo was born.

I still show up to every event myself. If you book the booth, you're booking me too — complete with terrible jokes and pencil smudges on my fingers.
